Hawaiian Pizza - Damien Lehkyj
6 Servings

INGREDIENTS:
2 x 335g 95.5% fat free thin pizza bases
140g tub pizza sauce
Vegetables of your choosing chopped up
1/4 pineapple, peeled, cut into 2cm pieces
150g button mushrooms, finely sliced
1 teaspoon dried mixed Italian herbs
1 cup
grated low-fat mozzarella cheese
100g baby spinach

METHOD:

Step 1
Preheat oven to 250°C. Place pizza bases onto 2 baking trays. Spread with sauce.

Step 2
Scatter your chosen vegetables, pineapple, mushrooms and herbs over bases. Sprinkle with cheese. 
Bake for 10 minutes, swapping trays after 8 minutes, or until bases are crisp.

Step 3
Cut pizzas into slices.

Emily - 7/10

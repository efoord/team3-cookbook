Prosciutto-wrapped Prawns - Added by Emily Foord
Serves 4

INGREDIENTS
12 medium green king prawns
1 garlic clove, crushed
2 tablespoons olive oil
6 slices prosciutto
olive oil cooking spray
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil

METHOD
1. Peel and devein prawns, leaving tails intact. Place prawns, garlic and half the oil in a medium bowl. 
Toss to coat prawns. Cut each slice of prosciutto in half lengthways. Wrap one piece prosciutto around 
each prawn, leaving the tails exposed. Place on a plate.
2. Spray a barbecue plate or chargrill with oil. Heat over medium-high heat. Cook prawns, turning, for 4 
to 5 minutes or until prawns are pink and cooked through.
3. Meanwhile, place lemon juice, basil and remaining oil in a small bowl. Whisk until combined. Arrange 
prawns on a platter. Drizzle with basil dressing. Serve.

RATING
Emily - 9/10

Tofu-skin-wrapped eggplant - Added by Emily Foord
Serves 4

INGREDIENTS
1 large eggplant
1 garlic clove, crushed
2 tablespoons olive oil
6 sheets of tofu-skin
olive oil cooking spray
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil

METHOD
1. Slice the eggplant.Place eggplant, garlic and half the oil in a medium bowl. 
Toss to coat the eggplant. Place on a plate.
2. Spray a barbecue plate or chargrill with oil. Heat over medium-high heat. Cook eggplant, turning, for 4 
to 5 minutes or until eggplants are tender and cooked through.
3. Wrap one sheet of tofu-skin around each eggplant slice.
4. Meanwhile, place lemon juice, basil and remaining oil in a small bowl. Whisk until combined. Arrange 
eggplants on a platter. Drizzle with basil dressing. Serve.

RATING
Emily - 6/10

COMMENTS
Emily - This is the vegetarian version of proscioutto-wrapped prawns

Salted Caramel Drops - Added by Emily Foord
Serves 24

INGREDIENTS
1/3 cup (75g) caster sugar
2/3 cup (160ml) cream
200g dark chocolate, chopped coarsely
200g milk chocolate

METHOD
1. Place sugar with 2 tbs. water in a small saucepan, over medium heat, stir until it dissolves. 
2. Bring to the boil, when golden brown, add the cream, stir over a low heat until toffee pieces melt. 
3. Remove from heat, stir in dark chocolate and ½ tsp. salt flakes. 
4. Quickly roll the mixture into teaspoon size balls, place on a paper-lined baking tray and freeze for 30 minutes. 
5. With 5 minutes to go, place milk chocolate in a large microwave safe bowl and melt on high, stirring every 20 seconds until smooth. 
6. Using two spoons, dip truffles into milk chocolate and return to the tray. 
7. Top each truffle with a sprinkle of 4-5 coarse salt flakes. Refrigerate to set.

RATING
Emily - 7/10

COMMENTS
